I am discovering that the print quality from CreateSpace can be highly variable. If you received a faulty print, you can have it replaced. If it was a kickstarter fulfillment, contact me (daniel@shiffman.net) directly. If you ordered it from CreateSpace, contact their customer support. If you ordered from Amazon.com, you'll have to contact them for a replacement. If you have any trouble at all, please contact me directly. I am trying to follow this closely in order to decide if I need to switch printers.

In addition, please send me photos of your copies along with the product number found in the very back of the book
